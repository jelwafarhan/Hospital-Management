About Us

Health Mission Hospital, established in 2014 was a thoughtful initiative from the renowned Rajagiri (CMI) group of institutions to offer quality and affordable medical care to the common man. The legacy of Rajagiri in pioneering a distinctive culture in the educational sector has helped them carve an exemplary medical institution in Kerala. The hospital is well laid out in a 40 acre green landscape in the serene setting of Chunangamvely, Aluva and is the closest facility to the Cochin International Airport. Our team of highly skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, and support staff, is committed to ensuring that you receive personalized and effective treatment. Whether you require emergency care, specialized treatment, or routine medical services, we are here to support you every step of the way.

Explore our website to learn more about our services, departments, and patient care programs. At Rajagiri - Hospital , your health and well-being are our top priority.
